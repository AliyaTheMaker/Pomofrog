🪷 Meet Lilypad 🪷

Meet Lilypad the Frog 🐸, your cheerful study buddy in Pomofrog™!

With Lilypad, you can choose from:

  • Pomodoro Timer – stay focused and productive.
  • Short Break – take a moment to stretch and recharge.
  • Long Break – grab a snack, use the bathroom, or relax for a bit.

You can start, pause, and reset the timer whenever you need.

✨ Special Timer Options

  • The heart button starts a quick 5-second timer.
  • The lavender lotus button lets you choose your own custom timer length.

📝 Froggy To-Do

Keep track of your tasks with the Froggy To-Do list! Add tasks, check them off when you're done, and clear completed items to stay organized.

🎮 Flappy Frog

Need a fun break? Play Flappy Frog during your short or long breaks and see how high you can score!

🐸 Grow Your Pond

Every time you complete a 25-minute Pomodoro session (Pomodoro mode only — not short break, long break, or 5-second timers!), a new frog appears in your pond! Visit Check Your Pond to see your growing collection of frog friends.

💰 Cash Out & Shop

Tap Cash Out Your Frogs! in your pond — for every 5 frogs, you earn 1 coin 💛! Then spend your coins in the Music 🎵 and Background 🖼️ shops to unlock new tunes and pretty backgrounds for Pomofrog!

Have fun staying focused, collecting frogs, and earning coins with Pomofrog™! 💗🐸🌸
